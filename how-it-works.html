Membership & Savings

How GIA Works

1
Join via an existing member

Applicants are introduced by a current member, approved by the Executive Committee, and added to the register.

2
Pay the entrance fee

A one-time membership fee of UGX 50,000 covers registration and administration.

3
Make monthly savings

Members contribute a minimum of UGX 50,000 per month. Additional savings are encouraged.

4
Access loans

Members in good standing may apply for loans guaranteed by savings and co-guarantors.

5
Participate in group investments

After one year, members decide on investment ventures with a two-thirds majority vote.

6
Share-out at cycle end

Remaining funds and profits are distributed proportionally at the end of each savings cycle.

Important: GIA is designed for long-term commitment. Early withdrawal may result in up to 60% of savings after loan settlement.
